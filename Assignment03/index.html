Winchester SX4

The Winchester SX4 is a semi-automatic shotgun that is designed for hunting and sport shooting. It is a versatile shotgun that is capable of firing a variety of different loads, from light target loads to heavy hunting loads. The SX4 features a gas-operated action that helps to reduce recoil and improve reliability. It also has a number of other features that make it a great choice for hunters and sport shooters, including a durable synthetic stock, a chrome-plated bore, and a ventilated rib barrel. Whether you are hunting waterfowl, upland game, or shooting clays, the Winchester SX4 is a great choice.

Features

  • Gas-operated action
  • Chrome-plated bore
  • Ventilated rib barrel
  • Durable synthetic stock
  • Available in a variety of finishes and configurations
