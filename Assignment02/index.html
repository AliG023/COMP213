Welcome to Conservation Areas In Ontario. Did you know Ontario's 36 Conservation Authorities own and protect a total of 150,000 hectares containing recreational lands, heritage sites, cultural site, wetlands and forests. 9.4% of the province is classified as Provicially Protected Area. The purpose of this is to protect important natural features as well as ecosystems. There are so many opportunites to adventure in these areas including hiking, canoeing, camping, snowshoeing, fishing, hunting and much more.

    Conservation Ontario has some great resources to help you get outside and experiencing all that Ontario has to offer.
